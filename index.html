Diabetes Care Guide

Follow these daily instructions to manage blood sugar levels better:

  • Check blood glucose at the same time each day and track readings.
  • Take prescribed medicines on time and do not skip doses.
  • Prefer low sugar, high-fiber meals and drink enough water.
  • Walk for at least 30 minutes daily and sleep on time.
  • Consult your doctor immediately if readings stay high for many days.

Cardiac Care Guide

Follow these cardiac care instructions for a healthier heart:

  • Monitor blood pressure regularly and keep a weekly record.
  • Reduce salty and oily foods; follow a heart-friendly diet.
  • Take cardiac medications exactly as prescribed by your doctor.
  • Walk or do light exercise for at least 30 minutes daily.
  • Do routine checkups and seek help immediately for chest discomfort.

Stomach Care Guide

Follow these stomach care instructions to reduce digestive discomfort:

  • Eat light meals at regular times and avoid overeating.
  • Reduce spicy, oily, and very acidic foods.
  • Drink enough water and include fiber-rich foods daily.
  • Take antacid or digestive medicines only as advised.
  • Visit a doctor if pain, acidity, or bloating continues.

Pain Relief Guide

Follow these pain relief tips to manage common body aches safely:

  • Use pain relief medicine only in the recommended dosage.
  • Apply hot or cold compress depending on the type of pain.
  • Stay hydrated and take proper rest for faster recovery.
  • Avoid heavy lifting and sudden strain during recovery.
  • Consult a doctor if pain continues for more than a few days.

Liver Care Guide

Use these liver care instructions to support better liver health:

  • Avoid alcohol and reduce processed or oily foods.
  • Drink enough water and follow a balanced diet.
  • Take liver supplements and medicines only as prescribed.
  • Do regular exercise and maintain healthy body weight.
  • Consult your doctor if fatigue, appetite loss, or jaundice appears.

Oral Care Guide

Follow these oral care instructions to maintain healthy teeth and gums:

  • Brush twice daily with fluoride toothpaste for at least 2 minutes.
  • Floss once a day to remove food particles between teeth.
  • Limit sugary snacks and rinse mouth after meals.
  • Replace your toothbrush every 3 months.
  • Visit your dentist regularly for cleaning and oral checkups.

Respiratory Care Guide

Follow these respiratory care instructions for better breathing health:

  • Avoid smoke, dust, and pollution-heavy areas as much as possible.
  • Use inhalers or respiratory medicines exactly as prescribed.
  • Drink warm fluids and stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Practice breathing exercises to improve lung function.
  • Visit a doctor quickly if cough or breathlessness persists.

Sexual Health Guide

Follow these basic sexual health instructions for better wellbeing:

  • Maintain personal hygiene and follow safe health practices.
  • Use prescribed wellness products only as directed.
  • Avoid self-medication for persistent symptoms.
  • Consult certified doctors for confidential guidance.
  • Do regular health checkups and stay informed.

Elderly Care Guide

Follow these elderly care instructions for safer daily health management:

  • Track blood pressure, sugar, and medicines daily.
  • Ensure balanced meals, hydration, and proper sleep.
  • Use mobility aids and prevent falls at home.
  • Keep regular doctor checkups and emergency contacts ready.
  • Encourage light exercise and social interaction every day.

Cold & Immunity Guide

Follow these tips to manage cold symptoms and boost immunity:

  • Stay hydrated and take warm fluids frequently.
  • Use prescribed cold-relief medicines on time.
  • Consume vitamin-rich foods and immunity supplements.
  • Take enough rest and avoid sudden weather exposure.
  • Consult a doctor if fever or cough continues.
